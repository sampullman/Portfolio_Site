Xenophobia is a Galaga clone in the works. It relies heavily on free graphics created by Kenney.

Arrow keys or w/a/s/d move the ship around, space shoots the main weapon, and shift fires a missile. Enemies have a small chance of dropping powerups. Toggle the volume or pause the game with buttons in the right hand corner. Your progress is saved each level if cookies are enabled, and you can restart a level if you lose all your lives.
